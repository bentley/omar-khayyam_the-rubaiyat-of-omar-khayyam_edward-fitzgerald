The Rubáiyát of Omar Khayyám
is based on the poetry of
Omar Khayyám
and was compiled and translated from Persian in 1859 by
Edward FitzGerald.
This ebook edition features illustrations completed in 1909 by
Edmund Dulac.

This ebook is based on a transcription produced in 2011 by
Fritz Ohrenschall, Sania Ali Mirza, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg.

The cover page is adapted from
the illustration of quatrain eleven of the first edition.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
